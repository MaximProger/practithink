What is PractiThink?
We use different bots and algorithms to create a personalized study plan for a student based on their current level and progress. It’s totally up to the account owner to adopt our suggestions, partially use them or completely ignore them. So you might think of it as a pacifist A.I. PractiThink challenges students with personalized tests that will test their knowledge. As they work their way through the system, our algorithm will suggest a variety of content through the personalized study plan to make sure they’re always working on exactly that which they need the most help.

MOST HELPFUL FAQS

Can non-US residents sell on PractiThink?
If your Product contains more than one file, you’ll need to combine all of the files into one folder. This folder becomes your Product file. If files from your Product are located in Google Docs™, Google Forms™, Google Sheets™ or Google Slides™, put all of the files into a single Google Drive folder. This Google Drive folder becomes your Product on the PractiThink Marketplace. If you don’t use Google Productivity Apps, combine all of the files into a single folder and then compress the folder into a ZIP file. Sometimes files get compromised when compressed. Please check files directly in the ZIP file before submitting your Product for review. Don’t forget to list all files in a product description and add preview images of the most interesting files in that folder.
